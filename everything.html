Description


When an interdimensional rupture unravels reality, an unlikely hero must channel her newfound powers to fight bizarre and bewildering dangers from the multiverse as the fate of the world hangs in the balance.


Directed By: Dan Kwan, Daniel Scheinert

Runtime: 2 hours 12 minutes (132 minutes)

Critic Reviews

"While this is one of the most intensely action-packed movies in recent memory, it's also sharply moving. And even beautiful."(Rich Cline)

"A great deal of energy and talent has been expended here on the idealisation of the family unit, and the quest for a United States that never existed."(Rich Gilbey)

"A pure firework display of technical bravado, wild invention, emotional storytelling, comedic genius, action mastery and outstanding performances, Everything Everywhere All At Once is everything cinema was invented for."(Ben Travis)
